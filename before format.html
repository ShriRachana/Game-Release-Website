Calling all Autobots, Decepticons, Maximals, and Predacons! Join Optimus Prime and the Earth Defense Command, who have crash-landed on a strange planet where multiple realities collide to form a massive planetary battlefield.

Assemble the ultimate team of Autobots and Decepticons from across time and space as you fight against a world of corrupted Transformers and their villainous overlords. Master intuitive touch controls in intense one-on-one combat, form mighty alliances and wage war against others, and create a fortified base to withstand the attacks of other players and house your riches.

With the Matrix of Leadership under your command, become the ultimate leader of the Transformers, and battle for supremacy in a planet full of danger, where friends become foes and foes becomes friends; in a world that is “More Than Meets the Eye.”

Installation Guides

STEP 1: Download Transformers

Click Download button to save the Transformers.apk file on your device.

NOTE: You must be connected to Kabam’s WiFi

STEP 2: Install File Manager app

1.Search for and install File Manager from the Google Play store.

NOTE: You must be connected to Kabam’s WiFi

STEP 3: Enable 3rd Party App Installation

1.Go to Settings on your phone and switch to the “general” tab, if needed.

2.Tap on the “Security” option.

3.Tick the checkbox next to the “Unknown Sources” option.

4.Confirm the warning message by tapping on “OK”.

STEP 4: Install Game (via File Manager)

1.Launch File Manager app (skip onscreen tutorial if prompted)

2.Select Downloads

3.Select and launch “transformers.apk” installer file (downloaded from Step 1) .

4.Select Install to install Transformers on your device.

STEP 5: Play Transformers

1.Launch Transformers app

2.Sign up using your Kabam email address: user@kabaminc.com
